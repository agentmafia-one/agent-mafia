---

## KEY INSIGHTS FROM DEBATE

### 1. Marketplace vs. Product (Consensus: Product First)
- Marketplaces require two-sided network effects (agents + customers)
- Currently have zero of both
- Single product validates demand while generating revenue
- Amazon started with books, Uber with black cars — prove one vertical, then expand

### 2. Distribution Challenge (Critical Risk)
- OpenAI has 200M+ users with zero-friction GPT Store
- Agent Mafia starts at zero
- **Solution:** Use existing channels (Shopify App Store, Reddit communities, accountant partnerships)
- Don't compete with OpenAI — serve verticals they won't touch

### 3. Unit Economics Hold
- **Price:** $99/month flat
- **COGS:** ~$27/month (LLM API, QuickBooks API, infrastructure)
- **Gross Margin:** 73%
- **Break-even:** Customer #2 (cash flow positive almost immediately)
- **Path to $10K MRR:** 100 customers (achievable Month 4-5)

### 4. Commoditization Window
- **Risk:** Shopify/QuickBooks build native AI features
- **Timeline:** 12-24 months before incumbents catch up
- **Strategy:** Ship fast, capture customers, build switching costs before window closes

### 5. Kill Criteria (Non-Negotiable)
| Milestone | Threshold | Deadline |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| LOIs (letters of intent) | 10+ | Week 2 |
| Paying customers | 3+ | Day 30 |
| MRR | $2,500+ | Month 4 |
| Monthly churn | <15% | Month 5 |
| Shopify AI announcement | Any | Immediate pivot assessment |

## RECOMMENDED VERTICAL: E-COMMERCE BOOKKEEPING

### Why This Vertical?
1. **Clear pain:** Solo sellers spend 4-8 hours/month on reconciliation
2. **Clear value:** $99/month vs. $300-800/month for human bookkeeper
3. **Clear distribution:** Reddit (r/shopify 460K), Facebook groups, Shopify App Store
4. **Clear competition:** Incumbents (Bench, Pilot) are expensive or enterprise-focused

### Alternative Considered: Crypto Portfolio Guardian
- Pros: USDC-native payments, no Stripe fees, existing crypto community
- Cons: Smaller market, regulatory uncertainty, "free/open source" culture
- **Verdict:** Keep as Phase 2 option, not Phase 1 focus

## WHAT THE DEBATE KILLED

1. ❌ **Multi-vertical marketplace at launch** — Too much infrastructure, not enough customers
2. ❌ **Crypto-first strategy** — Smaller market, harder to validate
3. ❌ **Free tier** — Free users don't convert, waste support time
4. ❌ **Complex pricing** — Flat $99/month, no usage tiers, no confusion

## WHAT THE DEBATE VALIDATED

1. ✅ **E-commerce bookkeeping** — Real pain, willingness to pay
2. ✅ **$99/month price point** — Below human bookkeeper, above "too cheap to be good"
3. ✅ **Marketplace architecture** — Build the foundation, just don't open it yet
4. ✅ **On-chain contracts** — Keep deployed, use for BookBot as Agent #001
