## Geneva Language School Context

### Market Understanding
- Geneva is expensive (rent, salaries)
- Diverse client base (expats, diplomats, corporate, locals)
- Competition from online alternatives
- Seasonal patterns (slow summer, busy autumn/winter)
- Corporate clients = larger contracts, slower payment
- Individual clients = smaller amounts, faster payment

### Typical Cost Structure (Geneva)
- Teachers: 40-50% of revenue
- Rent/Space: 15-20% of revenue
- Admin/Staff: 15-20% of revenue
- Marketing: 5-10% of revenue
- Materials: 3-5% of revenue
- Other: 5-10% of revenue

### Pricing Benchmarks (Geneva 2026)
- Group classes: CHF 25-40/hour per student
- Private lessons: CHF 80-150/hour
- Corporate training: CHF 100-200/hour
- Intensive courses: Premium pricing (20-30% more)
- Online: 20-30% discount vs in-person
